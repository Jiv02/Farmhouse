About Our Farmhouse

Nestled amidst the serene beauty of the countryside, The Farmhouse @Litte Falls offers a peaceful haven away from the stresses of city living. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature, breathe in the fresh air, and savor the flavors of organic, homegrown goodness.

Our farmhouse is family-owned and has been welcoming guests for over 20 years. We pride ourselves on providing a warm, welcoming environment where you can relax and reconnect with nature.
